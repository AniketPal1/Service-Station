HOW TO USE THE DASHBOARD
================================================================================

1. FIRST TIME VISITOR:
   → Go to index.html (homepage)
   → Click "Sign Up" or navigate to auth.html
   → Create new account with name, email, and password
   → You will be automatically signed in

2. EXISTING USER:
   → Go to auth.html (or link from homepage)
   → Click "Sign In"
   → Enter email and password
   → You will be redirected to dashboard.html

3. BOOKING A SERVICE:
   → From dashboard, click "Book Service" button
   → Or navigate to services.html from navigation
   → Click "Book Now" on any service
   → Fill in booking form (date, time, address, notes)
   → Confirm booking
   → Booking will appear in dashboard booking history

4. VIEWING BOOKINGS:
   → Go to dashboard.html
   → If logged in, scroll to "Your Bookings" section
   → All your bookings will be displayed with details
   → Click "Cancel" to cancel a booking
   → Click "Reschedule" for rescheduling option

5. MANAGING PROFILE:
   → Go to dashboard.html
   → Look for user profile card at the top
   → Click "View Profile" to see full details
   → Click "Logout" to sign out
